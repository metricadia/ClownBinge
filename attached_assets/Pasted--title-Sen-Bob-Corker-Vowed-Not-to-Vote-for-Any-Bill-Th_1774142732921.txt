It was in the bill he voted for.</p><p>Corker's defenders, including aides directly involved in the conference process, told CNN that he was not aware of the pass-through provision and that it did not drive his vote change. The argument was that with McCain unable to vote, Corker's no vote could have killed the entire bill, and he was unwilling to be the deciding vote against his party's signature legislative priority.
