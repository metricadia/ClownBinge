ARCHITECT VERDICT — THE SYSTEM IS NOW MATERIALLY STRONGER

The old gate had a fundamental alignment problem: the generator was trained to forbid things the scan had never heard of. Patterns like "Ultimately,", "In the end,", "enriches our understanding", "underscores", "emblematic", "pivotal" — all explicitly prohibited in the generator's constitution — had no corresponding scan check. An article could carry any of those and pass all 5 blocks.

That gap is now closed. The gate runs 27 distinct B1 checks across six rule families:

Family	Patterns
Superlatives / editorial adjectives	remarkable, significant, extraordinary, profound, monumental, groundbreaking, transformative, pivotal, damning, emblematic
Editorial phrases	stands as, cannot be overstated, far beyond mere, by any measure, at its core, cannot be ignored
Forbidden H2 headers	Legacy, Significance, Conclusion, Remarkable, Impact
Structural	body H1, em dash in visible text
Terminal + significance verbs	reflects/indicates/suggests/portrays/reveals at paragraph end; underscores
Editorial characterization	dead wrong, matters because, symbolic/symbolism/symbolically
Conclusion openers	Ultimately, / In the end, / Taken together,
AI malformations	enriches/deepens/broadens understanding, fills an important gap
N&FN ran through all 27 checks — 60/60 clean. Two real violations were found and fixed in the process ("by any measure" in CB-000327 and CB-000370). No false positives.
